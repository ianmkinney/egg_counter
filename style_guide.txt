# Eggs Up Grill - Comprehensive Style Guide

Based on my analysis of the Eggs Up Grill website, here is a complete style guide documenting all fonts, colors, and logos:

## **COLORS**

### Primary Colors
- **Teal/Turquoise** - Primary brand color used for header, footer, and accent sections (appears to be approximately #50B8B4 or similar)
- **White** - #FFFFFF - Background color for content sections

### Secondary Colors  
- **Bright Yellow/Gold** - Used for accent text like "WELCOME TO" and "COMFORT WITH EVERY CRACK OF AN EGG" (approximately #FDB913 or similar)
- **Orange** - Vibrant orange used for decorative accent boxes (approximately #FF9F1C)
- **Black** - Used for body copy text

### Additional Colors
- **Light Gray/Off-White** - Used for background sections
- **Teal Overlay** - Semi-transparent teal overlay on hero images

## **TYPOGRAPHY**

### Heading Fonts
- **Script/Cursive Font** - Used for main display text like "Eggs Up Grill," "Get To Know Us," "Own An Eggs Up Grill," "Our Menu," "Locations," "Catering," and "Order Now." This appears to be a flowing, handwritten-style script font (possibly similar to Allura, Pacifico, or a custom script)

### Body & Supporting Text Fonts
- **Sans-Serif Font** - Used for:
  - "WELCOME TO" (all caps, bold, yellow)
  - "COMFORT WITH EVERY CRACK OF AN EGG" (all caps, bold, orange)
  - Section headings like "CONTACT," "JOIN OUR TEAM," "NUTRITION GUIDE," "NEWS," "GIFT CARDS"
  - Subheadings like "Have Questions or Comments?" and "Egg'citing Opportunities" (in yellow)
  - Body text and descriptions
  - Button text
  - Appears to be a clean, modern sans-serif (possibly similar to Montserrat, Open Sans, or a custom sans-serif)

### Text Styles
- **All Caps** - Used for section headers and buttons
- **Mixed Case** - Used for body copy and subheadings
- **Bold Weight** - Used for headings and emphasis
- **Regular Weight** - Used for body text

## **LOGOS**

### Primary Logo
- **Main Logo** - "EGGS UP GRILL" text in white on teal background
  - Features white text in all caps
  - Has a yellow curved underline/smile beneath the text
  - Appears in the header navigation (centered)
  - Also appears in footer and mid-page sections

### Logo Variations
The logo appears in multiple contexts:
1. **Header Logo** - White text with yellow accent line on teal background
2. **Footer Logo** - Same styling as header
3. **Mid-page Logo** - Used in franchise section

### Brand Elements
- **Yellow Curved Line/Smile** - Signature element that appears under the logo text, representing a smile or sunny disposition
- The smile/curve element reinforces the friendly, welcoming brand personality

## **BUTTONS**

### Button Styles
- **Outlined Buttons** - White text with white border on teal background
  - Examples: "LEARN MORE," "SUBMIT A FORM," "VIEW JOB OPENINGS," "SIGN-UP"
  - Appear to have rounded corners
  - All caps text

- **Link Buttons** - Teal colored text for links like "Read More"

## **DESIGN PATTERNS**

### Decorative Elements
- **Torn Paper Edge** - Textured, torn paper effect between the "Get To Know Us" section and "Own An Eggs Up Grill" section
- **Textured Background** - Subtle texture applied to teal sections
- **Yellow Accent Lines** - Horizontal yellow divider lines used between sections on teal backgrounds

### Layout
- **Grid Layout** - Four-quadrant grid used for navigation sections (Our Menu, Locations, Catering, Order Now)
- **Image Overlays** - Text overlaid on hero images with teal color overlay
- **Center Alignment** - Logo and main headings centered

This style guide captures the warm, friendly, and approachable brand identity of Eggs Up Grill, with its signature teal and yellow color scheme, mix of playful script and clean sans-serif typography, and welcoming visual elements.[1][2]
